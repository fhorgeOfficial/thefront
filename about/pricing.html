Frequently Asked Questions

How can I pay for my certificate?

If you wish to view the exact price of your certificate, check out our Pricing page. If you require the use of a currency converter, please click here. You can purchase your certificate directly from the Fhorge Shop, simply click on the PDF/ Certificate/ Diploma icon you wish to purchase and it will be added to your cart.

What is Fhorge Premium Monthly?

Fhorge Premium Monthly is a monthly subscription service that allows you to upgrade your standard free Fhorge account to access our premium-range services. For a monthly subscription fee of €7.99 per month, Premium Monthly allows you to study 1000+ courses with absolutely no advertisements and also gives you exclusive monthly discounts that you can use towards any Certificate or Diploma available to you.

How can I upgrade to an Ad Free Fhorge Account?

Click on the Profile icon. Select Remove Ads - this will add the product to your Shopping Cart. Go to your Shopping Cart - this is the cart shaped icon at the top of the page. Insert a Voucher Code if you have one, click Continue.
